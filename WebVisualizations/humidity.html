Humidity


The humidity is high across the world at this time of the year, summer, represented by the numerous red-colored regions on the heatmap representing maximum or near-maximum humidity. The values seem especially high in places near the oceans. As you can see from the Fig 2, there is no correlation between humidity (%) and latitude. The heatmap was built to show the humidity across cities.
